🌱 My Journey

Schooling

My journey began at St. Xavier's Inter College, where I was always curious about learning and enjoyed exploring new things. Along with academics, I actively participated in extracurricular activities that helped shape my personality. I received awards for my sketching skills, earned certificates in basketball and throwball, and was proud to be a permanent member of my school's choir group. These experiences taught me creativity, discipline, teamwork, and the confidence to express myself.

College (Present)

After school, I started pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering at Sanskriti University, Mathura. My college journey introduced me to programming, and like many beginners, my first program was the classic "Hello, World!" in C++. Although I started coding in my first year, it was after completing my second year that I truly understood the importance of consistency. That realization became a turning point, motivating me to focus on Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) alongside web development to build a strong software engineering foundation.

For me, learning DSA is not just about solving coding problems or preparing for placements. My biggest motivation is to build applications that solve real-world problems and improve people's everyday lives. I regularly practice DSA on LeetCode, maintain my code on GitHub, and although I have solved around fifty problems so far, each one strengthens my problem-solving skills and way of thinking.

Alongside DSA, I have been exploring Full Stack Web Development. I have built several projects, including a personal portfolio website. To transform ideas into real products, I began my full-stack development journey with HTML and plan to continue learning CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, databases, and other technologies required to become a professional Full Stack Developer. Every new concept brings me closer to building meaningful solutions.

The rapid growth of Artificial Intelligence inspired me to start exploring AI as well. While I am still in the early stages, my long-term goal is to combine Full Stack Development with AI to create intelligent applications that make a real impact.

I enjoy identifying everyday problems and brainstorming solutions. Currently, I work as a Marketing Manager at PGMitra, where I am learning how technology, marketing, and teamwork come together to build products. Alongside this, I am developing ideas for future projects such as NuviSkin, an offline learning platform that provides free education without internet access, and a voice-guided healthcare application designed for parents with limited literacy, helping them monitor a child's health from birth to adulthood through an intuitive voice-based experience.

Today, as a third-year Computer Science Engineering student with a 9.05 CGPA, I continue strengthening my DSA skills, mastering Full Stack Web Development, exploring AI, building meaningful projects, and preparing for internships and placements. Beyond building a successful career, my greatest ambition is to create technology that solves real-world problems, improves lives, and leaves a positive impact on society.

For me, every line of code is not just a step toward becoming a better developer—it's a step toward becoming someone who builds solutions that truly matter.
